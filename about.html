THE STORY

It started in a co-working space in Coimbatore with three engineers, a whiteboard, and a shared frustration: enterprise software was too slow, too expensive, and too disconnected from real business needs.

Instead of months-long waterfall projects that delivered yesterday's solutions to tomorrow's problems, we built STACKLY around a single principle — ship fast, iterate faster, never compromise on quality.

"Great infrastructure shouldn't be a luxury. It should be the baseline."
