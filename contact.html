How quickly do you respond to enquiries? +
We respond to all messages within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, call us directly — our team is available during IST business hours.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises? +
Both. We have packages designed for early-stage startups that need infrastructure without the enterprise price tag, as well as full-scale engagements for large organisations.
Do you offer fixed-price projects or only retainers? +
We offer both models. Fixed-price works well for well-scoped projects. Retainers work better for ongoing development, support, or when requirements are evolving.
Can we sign an NDA before discussing our project? +
Absolutely. We're happy to sign NDAs before any substantive discussion. Just mention it in your message and we'll have paperwork ready before our first call.
What does the discovery call cover? +
The 30-minute call covers your current situation, goals, rough timeline, and budget. There's no pitch — just an honest conversation about whether we're the right fit for each other.
